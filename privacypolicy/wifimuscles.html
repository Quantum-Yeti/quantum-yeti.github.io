Privacy Policy for WiFi Muscles

Effective date: April 16, 2026

WiFi Muscles is a WiFi signal analysis app that helps users visualize and understand their WiFi connection in real time.

Information We Collect

WiFi Muscles does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app does not require account creation and does not transmit any user data to external servers.

Device and Network Information

The app may access local WiFi and network information (such as signal strength, SSID, and connection details) solely for the purpose of displaying WiFi analysis results on the device. This information is processed locally and is not stored or shared.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and use certain data for advertising purposes, such as device identifiers and advertising ID, in accordance with their privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not control or access this data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information. Any data used for advertising is managed directly by Google AdMob.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (for ads)

Security

Since no personal user data is collected or stored by WiFi Muscles, there is no risk of user data leakage from our app.
